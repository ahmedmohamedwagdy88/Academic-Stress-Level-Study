## 🎯 Objective
This project analyzes academic stress among students, focusing on:
- Peer pressure
- Academic pressure from home
- Study environment
- Coping strategies

## 💡 Key Insights
- **Peer Pressure vs Stress Index**  
  Stress levels rise consistently with higher peer pressure.  

- **Academic Pressure vs Stress Index**  
  Academic pressure from home shows a stronger correlation with stress than peer pressure.  

- **Study Environment vs Stress Index**  
  Students in peaceful environments report lower stress, while noisy/disrupted settings are linked to higher stress and variability.  

- **Coping Strategies**  
  - 63% rely on rational coping (analyzing and handling situations intellectually).  
  - 22% experience emotional breakdowns.  
  - 15% depend on social support.  

---

## 🎯 Implications
- Improve study environments to reduce stress.  
- Balance academic expectations from home.  
- Promote healthier coping mechanisms and expand social support networks.
